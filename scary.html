World's 50 Scariest People Named

Each Halloween, Cover Ghoul Magazine honors the World's 50 Scariest People. This season, Cover Ghoul's panel of editors has hand-picked a full house of frightening folks, including:

  • Martha Stewart
  • Dennis Rodman
  • Mick Jagger/ Keith Richards/Ron Wood/Bill Wyman/Charlie Watts (group award)
  • Pauley Shore
  • Darryl Davis
  • Kathi Lee Gifford
  • Jesse Helms
  • Roseanne (fifth straight year)
  • Burt Reynolds

Davis, a little-known Computational Cognitive Psychologist from Stafford Stafforshire, is a newcomer to this exclusive club of people voted "The Person I Would Least Like to be Alone With in an Elevator."

Cover Ghoul's panel was unanimously dazzled by Davis 's "effortless ability to spread panic in restaurants, stores, even at the Stafford Jail."

In recognition for being named one of the World's 50 Scariest People, Davis will be named honorary mayor of Ghost Town, USA, and will be featured in "Halloween XVII: Web of Horror."
